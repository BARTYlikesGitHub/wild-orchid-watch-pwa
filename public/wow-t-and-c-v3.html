Definitions

The WOW app has been set up to collect user obtained photographs of orchids and orchid habitats. The app has been developed under the Wild Orchid Watch citizen science project funded by Inspiring Australia, an Australian Government Initiative.

The app has been developed to allow the user to collect site information and capture photographs of orchids in-situ without disturbing the plant.

WOW utilises the iNaturalist platform as an image repository where images and supporting information (image metadata, site attribute and user information) will be stored. This will be accessible to registered users and managed by the WOW admin group.

Privacy Policy

The purpose of this policy is to tell you what kind of personal information WOW may gather about you when you use the WOW app and iNaturalist platform, how we may use that information, whether we disclose it to anyone, and how you can access this information.

Your right to privacy

In dealing with your personal information WOW must comply with the Commonwealth Privacy Act 1988  which regulates the collection, storage, quality, use and disclosure of personal information.

What is your personal information?

Personal information is information or an opinion about you. You do not need to be named in the information. It is only necessary that your identity can reasonably be ascertained from the information. Personal information is found not just in paper files, but also in such things as metadata, video recordings and photographs.

What personal information do we collect?

We will collect the following information from you when you register to use the WOW app: your full name, user name and email address.

We may collect the following information from you when you use the app and view or upload images into the iNaturalist platform:

·         information about the physical site where the images are being captured, including time, date and GPS coordinates when you upload images and actively conduct an orchid survey using the app

·         information about the orchid observation or orchid survey activity where the images/activity are being captured

·         user content that you submit for WOW (e.g. orchid photos, orchid habitat photos, habitat and site variables)

·         your IP (internet protocol) address or host name when you visit the iNaturalist platform

·         the date and time you visited the iNaturalist platform

·         the pages or images you attempted to view or download, and whether those pages or images were displayed

·         the data you attempted to view or download, and whether the requested data were displayed

·         the device and operating system you are using

·         whether you have previously visited iNaturalist platform (only if you accept cookies).

We will not collect other personal information from you. If you upload images that contain people or other identifiable information to the image repository this information will be removed and deleted either entirely, or from user access and visibility.

Why are you collecting my personal information?

The purpose of collecting your personal information is to help administer the app and help validate any field data that has been collected with the images that users submit.

The app forms an integral part of the WOW project and the field survey component developed to enable citizen scientists to collate information on wild orchid populations, their locations, habitat and site variables, phenology, and disturbance issues threatening them.

We use your personal information to deliver our services to you so that you can participate in the full experience of the application.

In addition to some of the specific uses of information we describe in this Privacy Policy, we may use information that we receive to:

·         help you efficiently access your information after you sign in

·         remember information so you will not have to re-enter it during your visit or the next time you visit the app and image repository

·         provide, improve, test, and monitor the effectiveness of the app and image repository

·         develop and test new products and features

·         diagnose or fix technology problems

Is providing my personal information voluntary?

Providing the personal information described above is voluntary, yet if you do not provide this information you cannot use the WOW web app or the WOW iNaturalist portal.

Will you disclose my personal information to any person?

WOW may disclose your personal information including your first name, surname and login details (username, email and password) to the WOW administrators, and the state/territory environment data agencies. The purpose of this disclosure is to administer the app and to validate field data.

We will not otherwise disclose your personal information to any person unless you consent or in circumstances where such disclosure is permitted under the Commonwealth Privacy Act 1988.

What do we do with your images?

Images collected and submitted using WOW will end up in a WOW managed image repository using the iNaturalist platform. This function of the iNaturalist platform is only accessible to WOW personnel that are managing WOW.

Images and supporting information collected using WOW may be used for image analysis purposes to extract quantitative information. Images may also be used to monitor the condition of the site captured in the images or used for reporting purposes.

What about links to other websites?

This privacy policy applies solely to information collected by the app and image repository.

The app and image repository may contain links to other sites. We make no representations or warranties as to the privacy practices of any site operated by a third party and are not responsible for the privacy policies of such sites.

We recommend that you examine the privacy policy for all third party websites you visit to understand their procedures for collecting, using and disclosing personal information.
